About Snoop Dogg

Snoop Dogg, born Calvin Cordozar Broadus Jr., is an American rapper, singer, and entrepreneur. Known for his laid-back, charismatic style, Snoop Dogg has become one of the most influential figures in hip-hop and pop culture. With a career spanning over two decades, he has released numerous albums, collaborated with artists across genres, and even ventured into acting and business.

Top Albums

  • Doggy Style (1993)
  • The Doggfather (1996)
  • Tha Last Meal (2000)
  • R&G (Rhythm & Gangsta): The Masterpiece (2004)
  • Coolaid (2016)

Fun Facts

  • Snoop Dogg is a passionate marijuana advocate.
  • He is an avid fan of sports, particularly football and soccer.
  • Snoop has appeared in movies, TV shows, and has his own cooking show with Martha Stewart.
